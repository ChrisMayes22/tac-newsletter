Meet the Team

COACH DAVID
David Pérez is the founder and head coach at TAC. As the “architect,” he trains every coach for at least 100 hours in TAC’s strategies, methods, and philoso phy before assigning coaches to students. As the head coach, David meets with students, TAC families for initial consultations, and is constantly developing new material to keep TAC at the forefront of innovative and efficient test prep and academic coaching. David also participates in detailed planning of every session for all students, providing an individualized, long-term plan for test-day success.
COACH CHANCE
Chancellor is one of TAC’s most devoted coaches, having taken the official ACT test 9 times! His persistence paid off when he raised his scorefrom a 25 on his first test to a 35 on the final test. His personal experience overcoming testing challenges will prove invaluable to TAC students. Chancellor received his BA summa cum laude in Religious Studies from Oklahoma Baptist University and moved to the Raleigh area to pursue his Masters & PHD in Divinity from Southeastern. Chancellor is also an experienced academic coach, as he tutored classmates in english, writing, science, and math courses throughout college and beyond.
COACH DAN
Dan is a Wake Forest native who graduated from NC State with a B.A. in Public and Interpersonal Communication. Dan has always had an affinity for academic coaching, as he spent much of his time tutoring local middle and high school students in a wide range of academic subjects, including AP English & Calculus. Nowadays, Dan’s favorite academic subject to teach is college test prep as he is inspired by seeing his students produce tremendous score improvement and achieve their goals. In his spare time, he is constructing a high school level Intro to Communication curriculum that he hopes to get implemented by local school districts in the near future.
